ABOUT FILIPINOS



The food is great... a must try
-Rice is a big part of their diet
-Some popular dishes include chicken adobo and pancit





Filipinos are very prideful of themselves and their country
-When it comes to a boxing fight invovling the boxer, Manny Pacquiao, crime rates actually drop in the Philippines
-There's a saying that goes, ”It’s more fun in the Philippines”





They loveee to sing
-Karaoke is where Filipinos can express their talents
-Through this, they can get a little competitive





Christmas is a big deal!
-Decorations and preparations start as early as August or September
-This displays that they are also quite religious





Respect is widely displayed
-Blessing your elders is a practice done by taking your elder's hand and raising the back of their hand to gently touch your forehead
-When you refer to someone older, you call them your "Ate"(for females) or "Kuya"(for males) as a sign of respect
